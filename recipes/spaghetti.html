Spaghetti recipe

Who doesn't love a warm plate of classic spaghetti! One of my families all time favorite meals and sure to be loved by everyone.

Ingredients

  • Â½ lb Ground beef I use the Prego sauce with meat so I only use a half pound in my recipe.
  • 1 small onion diced
  • 1 tbsp garlic minced or powder
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp parsley
  • 2 O of noodles when you place your thumb and pointer finger together to form an OK.
  • 4 cups spaghetti sauce

Steps

  1. Heat pan on medium high heat.
  2. Finley dice your onion.
  3. In your heated pan place ground beef, diced onion.
  4. Brown ground beef until all pink is gone and the onion is translucent.
  5. In a large pot place in sauce and all seasonings.
  6. Once beef is fully cooked, drain and mix into sauce.
  7. Let sauce heat until all flavors are combined. (for a richer flavor let stew for several hours)
  8. Bring water to a rolling boil in a large pot.
  9. Cook noodles until tender then drain.
  10. Once pasta is drained serve warm and top with Parmesan cheese.
